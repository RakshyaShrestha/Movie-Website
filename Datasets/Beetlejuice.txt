Title: Beetlejuice
Genre: Fantasy, Comedy, Horror
Year: 1988
Director: Tim Burton
Starring: Michael Keaton, Alec Baldwin, Geena Davis, Winona Ryder, Catherine O'Hara, Jeffrey Jones

After Barbara and Adam Maitland, a young couple, die in a car accident, they find themselves stuck as ghosts in their own home. When the unbearable Deetz family moves in, the Maitlands attempt to scare them away without success. In their desperation, they enlist the help of an obnoxious and mischievous bio-exorcist named Beetlejuice. However, Beetlejuice's unpredictable nature soon makes things worse, and the Maitlands must deal with the chaos that ensues. The film is a mix of dark humor and supernatural elements, ultimately focusing on the struggle between the living and the afterlife, and the meaning of home and family.
