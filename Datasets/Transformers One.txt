Title: Transformers One
Genre: Animation, Action, Sci-Fi
Year: 2024 (upcoming)
Director: Josh Cooley
Starring: Chris Hemsworth, Brian Tyree Henry, Scarlett Johansson, Keegan-Michael Key, Jon Hamm, Laurence Fishburne

"Transformers One" is an animated prequel that explores the origin story of the Transformers on their home planet, Cybertron. The film focuses on the relationship between a young Optimus Prime and Megatron, detailing how they went from allies to adversaries, leading to the eventual division of their society into Autobots and Decepticons. Set in a time before the events of the live-action Transformers movies, this film delves into the backstory of the iconic rivalry, highlighting the events that caused the civil war that would change the fate of their world forever.
