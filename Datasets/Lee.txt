"Lee" is a biographical film that follows the life of Lee Miller, a former model who became a trailblazing war correspondent during World War II. The movie explores her journey from her early years in the fashion world to her transformation into a renowned photographer covering the frontlines of war. Lee Miller's courage and determination take her through some of the most harrowing events of the 20th century, documenting the horrors of the war and challenging the limitations placed on women of her time. The film highlights her contributions as an artist and her pursuit of truth amidst the chaos of conflict.
