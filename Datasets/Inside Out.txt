Title: Inside Out
Genre: Animation, Adventure, Comedy
Year: 2015
Director: Pete Docter, co-directed by Ronnie del Carmen
Starring: Amy Poehler, Phyllis Smith, Richard Kind, Bill Hader, Lewis Black, Mindy Kaling

The story takes place inside the mind of an 11-year-old girl named Riley as she moves to a new city. Her emotions—Joy, Sadness, Anger, Fear, and Disgust—navigate her experiences and help her adjust to the changes in her life. As Riley faces challenges in her new environment, Joy tries to maintain her happiness while learning to understand the value of other emotions, especially Sadness. Together, they embark on a journey through Riley’s mind, exploring her core memories and trying to help her cope with her new reality.
